An AI-powered data sanitization engine

Redac AI is an open-source tool for detecting secrets, finding PII, and eliminating false positives. Built on TruffleHog, Piicatcher, and FastAPI.

Why Redac AI?

Open Source

Redac AI is completely open source under the AGPL-3.0 license. You can audit the code, contribute improvements, and use it freely in your projects. No vendor lock-in, no hidden costs.

Production Ready

Battle-tested on repositories with millions of commits. Deployed in production by security teams at companies ranging from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises.

Extensible

Built with a plugin architecture that makes it easy to add custom detectors, integrate new data sources, or extend the AI classification rules to match your security policies.

Community Driven

Active development community with regular updates, responsive maintainers, and a growing ecosystem of plugins and integrations contributed by security professionals worldwide.
